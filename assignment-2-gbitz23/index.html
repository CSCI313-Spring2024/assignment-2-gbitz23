Biography

I'm from Dickinson, ND, which is a city about 4 hours east of Fargo. I play lots of golf in the summer and basketball in the winter. I enjoy spending time with my friends and my family, usually playing board/card games, playing video games, or just hanging out.

I am a Mathematics major and this is the last class I need to take to complete my Computer Science minor. Coming into college, I knew I loved math but I didn't really have an idea of what I wanted to do with it. I took CSCI 161 as one of the required courses for Gen Ed and I really loved it. I had never had any previous experience with coding or computers but I learned quickly with the help of Dr. Kotala and a couple of buddies who had coded before. I decided since CS has a close relation with Math, that it would be beneficial to pursue a minor in it and use those skills for my future career.

After college, I'll most likely go into Data Science or Data Analytics, as I've always had an analytical mind. It would be awesome to work for a sports organization of some sort, specifically golf or basketball. I hope to stay close to home in ND, either in Fargo or my hometown of Dickinson.
